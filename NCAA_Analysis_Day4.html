Florida's elimination is catastrophic for the pool. 123 brackets had Florida advancing here. Sunday's other big mover: Tennessee over Virginia killed 46 future picks. 73 brackets had Virginia in the Sweet 16, but only 35 of those had Virginia advancing further. The pool was heavily tilted toward Virginia, so Tennessee's win reshuffled the middle of the standings significantly.
